EEE

Electrical and Electronics Engineering is a very broad spectrum of Engineering giving exposure to the fields of Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Power Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Engineering and basics of Civil Engineering. It mainly concentrates on Electrical and Electronics Engineering ranging from the fundamentals of both to all aspects about power generation, power transmission, power distribution, power utilization, analysis of power system, Analog, Digital and Power Electronics Engineering, Drives and Control, Control systems, Electrical Machines including single and three phase AC, DC and Special Machines, Design of Electrical machines, System design, Digital signal processing etc.

ECE

Established in the year 2004 with an intake of 60 students , the department offers UG programme and carries out academic activities with adequate faculty, well equipped laboratories/workshops and students admitted on merit basis through Common Entrance Examination of Kerala.

CSE

Department of Computer Science was established in the year 2004. The strength of the department is the availability of well experienced faculty and supporting staff. The major objective of the department is to help the students achieve their goals by providing in-depth knowledge in fundamental courses and getting them exposed to latest technologies. It also aims to improve the personality of students by making them part of different training programmes.
